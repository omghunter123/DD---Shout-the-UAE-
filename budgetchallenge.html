The UAE Budget Challenge

How the Game Works

The Budget Challenge is a fun way to test your money skills. You are given a monthly salary and have to make smart spending decisions while avoiding common money traps.

Example Round

Imagine you earn AED 10,000. You need to spend wisely on rent, bills, food, transport, and leisure without overspending.

Your Turn!

Enter your salary and fill in your expenses. The goal: save *at least 20%* while still living comfortably.
