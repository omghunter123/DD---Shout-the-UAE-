UAE Money Tips

Tips to save money

  1. Use discount apps – Apps like The Entertainer, Cobone, and Groupon can cut your restaurant and activity costs almost in half.
  2. Shop during sales – UAE has huge sales like Dubai Summer Surprises and Abu Dhabi Big Sale, where prices drop a lot if you wait for the right time.
  3. Switch to Nol/metro/bus – Public transport is cheaper than using taxis, and it saves you a lot over time.
  4. Avoid daily small expenses – Things like buying snacks, karak, or delivery every day slowly add up, so try reducing them.
