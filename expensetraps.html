Money Traps to Watch Out for in the UAE

Common Spending Traps

In the UAE, it’s super easy to overspend without noticing. Luxury malls, online shopping, expensive restaurants, and forgotten subscriptions are the biggest traps.

Simple Ways to Avoid Overspending

  1. Set a budget and track everything you spend
  2. Don’t buy things instantly — wait and think
  3. Compare prices before buying anything expensive
  4. Use discounts, sales and cashback apps properly
  5. Stay away from high-interest loans and credit card debt

Places That Can Cost You More

Upscale malls can make you overspend without noticing
Online stores push impulse buying through limited-time deals
Eating out at fancy restaurants adds up fast

Hidden Costs to Watch

Delivery fees, service charges, car maintenance, and auto-renewing subscriptions are small costs that stack up quickly in the UAE.

Different Types of Expense Traps

  1. Impulse buying — buying without planning
  2. Unused subscriptions — money leaving your account quietly
  3. Luxury lifestyle — spending for image, not need
  4. Debt trap — depending on credit cards or loans
  5. Tourist traps — overpriced attractions and shops
